1st Screen: The main part of the screen is the front camera view of your chat 
buddy, on the upper right corner you can see your own front camera view. 
On the bottom of the screen you can start chatting with your chat buddy trough text, 
and you can also see some info about them on the top of the screen when opening text. 
You can swipe right to start talking with the next chat buddy,
or swipe left to open your profile configuration.

2nd Screen: In this screen you have multiple options to set your preferences 
and add some information bout yourself to better match with other chat buddies. 
You can swipe right to start chatting with others.
